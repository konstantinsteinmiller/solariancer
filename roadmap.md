# Solariancer — Retention Roadmap

All retention items from the original spec are now shipped. This file lives on as a record of what's in the game and a
parking spot for future ideas.

## Now shipped (for reference)

- ✅ **#1 Solar Flares** — windowed ×4 heat events with warning, runs every 90–180s.
- ✅ **#2 Combo Meter** — chain ripe feeds within 5s of each other. 3 chained = ×2 for 10s, 5 = ×3.
- ✅ **#3 Streak Badge + Sun Skins** — daily-feed streak persisted across sessions, drives sun-palette progression. Plus
  8-stage star-type cycle (G→K→M→Red Giant→Blue Dwarf→White Dwarf→Brown Dwarf→Neutron) on each 1000+ heat clear.
- ✅ **#4 Comets** — fast cross-screen visitors every 50–90s. First grab pays out heat + star matter.
- ✅ **#5 Black Hole Event** — every 10 ripe feeds, a transient black hole spawns and pulls everything for 6s. Survive
  without losing ripe bodies → bonus heat + star matter. Bodies dragged into the event horizon are consumed → +1 star
  matter each.
- ✅ **#6 Constellation puzzles** — skill-tested side objectives running parallel to missions. Three puzzles rotate (
  Crowd Master / Comet Hunter / Solo Survivor), 45-second windows, 30s cooldown between. Reward: +2 ✦ Star Matter and a
  flashy popup. PuzzleBadge in the HUD top-right under MissionBadge.
- ✅ **#7 Supernova prestige** — Solar Class system. Eligible at `1M × 2^solarClass` lifetime heat. Resets heat / stage /
  upgrades, keeps Star Matter / Streak / unlocks. Each rank adds **+5% to all heat earnings forever**. Solar Class chip
  on the StageBadge once ranked. Confirmation modal in the upgrade screen.
- ✅ **#8 Sun-skin selector** — manual override of the auto stage palette. Auto-unlocks as the stage cycles. Lives in
  Options → General.
- ✅ **#9 Quick missions** — 3-minute heat goal that scales with stage + Fusion / Cosmic Forge levels. MissionBadge in
  HUD top-right (below Streak). On success, modal lets the player pick 1 of 3 random rewards (+1000 Heat / +5 ✦ Star
  Matter / ×2 Heat for 60s / ×3 Combo for 30s / Ripen All).
- ✅ **#10 Trail palette customization** — 4 unlockable palettes (rainbow / ember / plasma / aurora) tied to milestones.
  Selectable in Options → General.
- ✅ **#11 Sound dynamics** — synthesised heat-hum drone, black-hole rumble, tutorial click track, ripe-thud pitch shift.
- ✅ **#12 Tutorial part 2** — card-only advanced tutorial. Triggers once after the player's 5th ripe feed. Teaches Crowd
  ×3, Combo chains, Close-to-Sun band, and event rewards.
- ✅ **Cosmic Forge** — first cosmic-tier upgrade (paid in Star Matter). +25% global heat per level, max 5.
- ✅ **Fusion Stabilizer (open-ended)** — no max level. Staircase per-level scaling: levels 1–15 add 5% each, 16–30 add
  1.25% each (¼×), 31+ add 0.625% each (⅛×).
- ✅ **Constellation puzzle pool expansion** — added 5 new puzzle types (Heat Wave, Chain Master, Triple Star, Forge
  Sprint, Pristine) bringing the rotation to 8.

## Future / unscoped ideas

- **Per-stage objective layer** — every Nth stage spawns a "boss" body (rogue planet) the player has to handle.
- **Co-op / async leaderboard** — best-session heat or longest combo streak per cosmetic skin.
- **Daily challenge** — fixed seed + scoring board.
