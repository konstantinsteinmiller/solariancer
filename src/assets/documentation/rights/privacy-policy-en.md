Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Protecting your privacy is a major concern for us. This Privacy Policy informs you about how we handle information
within our mobile application, Chaos Battle Arena.

2. No Data Collection

The core principle of our app is data minimization.

    Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personal data (such as name, email address, IP address, or location).

    Usage Data: We do not use any analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) to evaluate your gaming behavior.

    Device Information: We do not access your device identifiers (IDFA/AAID), contacts, photos, or camera.

3. Storage of Game Progress

All progress within the game (e.g., high scores or unlocked levels) is stored locally on your device only. We have no
access to this data. If you delete the app, this local game progress will also be removed.

4. Permissions

The app does not require any special permissions (such as access to location or contacts) to function properly.

5. Third Parties and Links

The app contains no third-party advertising and no links to social networks. No data exchange with external servers
takes place.

6. User Rights

Since we do not store any data about you, we cannot provide information about stored data, nor can we delete or correct
it—quite simply because it does not exist in our system.
